Filter Club

An open community of people who ❤️ brewed specialty coffee.

Founded in 2017 by a group of 🇭🇺 Budapest-based coffee geeks, with the goal to learn as much as we can about coffee by ⚙️ brewing, 👅 tasting and 🤗 sharing our ☕️ experiences.

🦸‍♀️ Our Mission

We strive to both:

  • 🤔🌍 think globally, sharing and discussing whatever coffee knowledge we come across
  • 🎭📍 and act locally (😅), organizing coffee tasting events and coffee competitions in our cities

🖖 Join Us

Membership is free and open to anyone. Filter Club is community-owned and maintained: anyone is welcome to contribute their attention and resources to building it.

If you don't have a Filter Club in your city yet (chances are you don't) and would like to create one 🙏 connect with us 👇 and we'll be happy to help you get started.
